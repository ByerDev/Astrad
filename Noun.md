```
(Number)(Article)((Gender))[Noun](Case)
```

### Ablative

## Articles
The article is simply a prefix to the noun. As in english, there are two types: indefinite and definite articles:

| indefinite | definite | none |
| ---------- | -------- | ---- |
| v-         | f-       | --   |

## Examples
- "I am a protogen" -> "chä vtsĉhʷikt"
